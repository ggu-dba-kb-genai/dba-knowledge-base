Contribute to the DBA Knowledge Base

Share your session notes, completed assignments, or class worksheets. Every contribution is reviewed, credited to you, and woven into the knowledge graph.

In scope

  • Session notes from DBA lectures
  • Your own completed assignments
  • Worksheets given in class
  • Session PDFs shared by GGU / UpGrad
  • Cohort group project outputs

Out of scope

  • External articles, papers, blog posts
  • Reference book chapters or extracts
  • Content from other programs
  • Opinions not grounded in course material
  • AI-generated content you haven't reviewed
