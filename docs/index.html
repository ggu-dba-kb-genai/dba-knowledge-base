The six courses

The doctoral arc — from the broader digital ecosystem to the technical core, to responsible deployment.

C1

Emerging Digital Technologies

IoT, cloud & HPC, neuromorphic computing, AR/VR/XR, RPA, digital twins, blockchain — and their strategic integration with AI.

C2

Foundations of ML & AI

Classical machine learning and statistical modelling — supervised & unsupervised learning, regression, clustering, time-series, model evaluation.

C3

Deep Learning

Neural networks and modern deep architectures, from fundamentals through training, optimisation and representation learning.

C4

Generative AI & Pretrained Models

Large language models, pretraining and fine-tuning, transformers, and the generative-AI toolchain.

C5

AI Project Design

Designing, scoping and executing AI initiatives — from research problem identification to delivery in a business context.

C6

Responsible AI

Fairness, accountability, transparency, security and governance — deploying AI responsibly at organisational scale.

Built on the Open Knowledge Format

Every course, session, concept and reference is a markdown file with structured front-matter and cross-links — portable, diff-able, and version-controlled. The interactive map and this site are generated from it.
