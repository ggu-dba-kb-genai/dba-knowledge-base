The **Emerging Digital Technologies (C1)** course is designed as the foundational gateway for the Doctor of Business Administration (DBA) program, bridging the gap between technical innovation and strategic business leadership. Recognizing that artificial intelligence cannot exist in a vacuum, the course provides a comprehensive exploration of the broader digital ecosystem. It traces the digital evolution from raw data gathering at the physical edge to high-performance centralized processing and secure decentralized coordination.

Throughout the course, students are introduced to a wide spectrum of paradigm-shifting technologies. These include the Internet of Things (IoT), Cloud and Cloud-Native Computing, High-Performance Computing (HPC), [Neuromorphic and Hardware Acceleration](../concepts/neuromorphic-and-hardware-acceleration.md), Immersive AR/VR/XR Systems, Robotic and Intelligent Process Automation (RPA/IPA), Digital Twins, [AI Security and Robustness](../concepts/ai-security-robustness.md), and [Decentralized AI and Blockchain](../concepts/decentralized-ai-and-blockchain.md). Special emphasis is placed on strategic integration, [Model Compression and Optimization](../concepts/model-compression-optimization.md) for edge deployment, and [Agentic AI and Autonomous Systems](../concepts/agentic-ai-autonomous-systems.md). The course's arc is intentionally designed to transition students from passive observers of technology into scholarly researchers who can identify, evaluate, and implement cutting-edge frameworks to drive organizational disruption, operational efficiency, and sustainable business value.
