# Course

* [C2: Foundations of ML & AI](c2-foundations-ml-ai.md) - A comprehensive technical foundation in classical machine learning and statistical modeling for executive decision-making.
* [C3: Deep Learning](c3-deep-learning.md) - A comprehensive course covering neural networks, backpropagation, and state-of-the-art architectures from vision models to sequence-to-sequence networks.
* [C4: GenAI Pretrained Models](c4-genai-pretrained-models.md) - A comprehensive course on generative artificial intelligence, exploring pretrained models, transformers, LLM training, prompt engineering, RAG, stable diffusion, and multi-agent workflows.
* [C5: AI Project Design](c5-ai-project-design.md) - A strategic course bridging the gap between algorithmic technicalities and business execution, detailing AI project strategy, prioritisation, requirements engineering, sourcing, and operational economics.
* [Emerging Digital Technologies (C1)](c1-emerging-digital-technologies.md) - A comprehensive doctoral course exploring key emerging digital technologies—including IoT, Cloud, HPC, Neuromorphic Computing, AR/VR, RPA, Cyber Security, and Blockchain—and their strategic integration with AI to solve complex business problems.
* [Responsible AI (C6)](c6-responsible-ai.md) - A executive-focused course exploring algorithmic ethics, AI governance, practical system design, and the Darwin Framework for successful AI product lifecycles.
