**Course:** Emerging Digital Technologies (C1) · **Type:** Individual report ·
**Length:** 2,000–2,500 words, APA format · **Weight:** 20 marks (50% of overall grade)

## Summary

Each student selects an organization (e.g., a large retailer, automaker, streaming
platform, or industrial firm) that has used one or more **emerging technologies** to
significantly transform its operations, value chains, or customer engagement, and writes
an individual analytical report. The brief asks the report to address the **nature of the
transformation**, **the technologies used and how they were deployed**, the **strategic
rationale and organizational impact**, the **risks, challenges, and ethical
considerations**, and the **ROI or measurable outcomes** of the initiative. A compliant
APA thesis template is provided as a starting point.

## Deliverable

A single APA-formatted report (2,000–2,500 words) submitted as a PDF.

## Rubric (20 marks)

| Criteria (5 each) | Excellent (4–5) | Good (3–4) | Fair (2–3) | Poor (0–2) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Clarity of Business Case | Clearly explains transformation, context, and relevance | Explains case well with some gaps | Somewhat vague; lacks context | No clear focus or business case |
| Technology Integration Analysis | Deep dive into 1–2 technologies with strong link to outcomes | Covers technologies well but lacks depth | Superficial discussion of tech | Technology mention is vague or incorrect |
| Strategic & ROI Implications | Evaluates strategic rationale and quantifies impact | Strategic points made but light on metrics | Minimal strategy, no data | No strategic alignment or value |
| Structure & Research | Well-organized, cited, professional | Mostly structured, with few errors | Disorganized, missing citations | Lacks structure or research base |
