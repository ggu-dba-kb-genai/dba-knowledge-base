**Course:** AI Project Design & Execution (C5) · **Type:** Team capstone (2–5 members) ·
**Total Marks:** 50 · **Presentation:** 12 min + 8 min board Q&A

## Summary

The final integrative capstone asks each team to act as an **Enterprise AI Leadership Team**
presenting to an **Executive Investment and Governance Board**. Teams pick their own AI
initiative and must continuously defend *why it should exist* and *why it can survive
operationally at enterprise scale*. Evaluation rewards **strategic clarity, research
justification, execution realism, operational feasibility, governance maturity, deployment
viability, QA/operational readiness, economics and value realization, and executive
defensibility** — not futuristic-sounding ideas, buzzwords, or architectural complexity.

## Required sections

Executive & research titles (A); strategic problem framing (B); 3–5 measurable research
objectives (C); research questions mapped to objectives (D); hypotheses (E); literature /
industry justification (F); AI opportunity prioritization — quick win vs strategic bet (G);
optional BRD/PRD-lite requirements (H); delivery & execution roadmap with stage gates (I);
Build vs Buy vs Partner vs Platform sourcing decision and TCO (J); deployment strategy —
managed API vs platform vs containerized, cloud/on-prem/hybrid, data residency, scalability
(K); readiness assessment — data/workflow/integration/governance, blockers, adoption risks
(L); QA, evaluation & release readiness — scenario coverage, edge cases, rollback,
human review (M); budgeting, unit economics & value realization with continue/pause/kill
thresholds (N); and a final executive recommendation — scale / controlled rollout / redesign
/ delay / reject (O).

## Mandatory deliverables

Executive slide deck (PPT/PDF); Executive AI Research-to-Execution dossier (Word/PDF);
architecture diagrams; value-realization dashboard; an optional governance & risk appendix;
and a mandatory one-page board memo.

> A strong capstone is judged by how logically, realistically, and defensibly the AI
