**Course:** Generative AI & Pretrained Models (C4) · **Type:** Group · **Total Marks:** 50 ·
**Pitch:** ≤ 10 slides for the elevator pitch

## Summary

Each group solves a business problem using the generative-AI techniques learned in the
course (a self-chosen problem or one from the assignment set). The team identifies a
**challenging problem**, articulates a **vision and mission**, arrives at a **unique
solution proposition using GenAI**, presents a **product roadmap**, and performs a
**return-on-investment analysis**. Preference is given to unique solution designs and
patentability aspects.

## Evaluation factors (points)

- Well-defined and complex problem chosen — **5**
- GenAI solution design — **10**
- Product roadmap aligned to the vision and its implementability — **10**
- Return-on-investment analysis — **10**
- Quality of the report and presentation (structure, flow; elevator pitch ≤ 10 slides) — **10**
- Unique aspects: potential for patent / publishable reports / blogs / VC-presentable — **5**

## Rubric

| Criterion | Good | Satisfactory | Needs Improvement | Did not address |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Well-defined and complex problem chosen | Shortlisted a strong problem with clear objectives. | Problem is clear but may not suit GenAI. | Problem statement is not clear. | — |
| Overall GenAI solution design | Approach clearly described, plus a clear success-estimation method. | Either approach or success-evaluation method unclear. | Neither approach nor evaluation method clearly described. | — |
| Product roadmap & implementability | Clear roadmap (short term to ~3 years); technically correct solution. | Some gaps in technical evaluation but roadmap is clear. | Not technically correct; roadmap unconvincing. | — |
| Return-on-investment analysis | Clear analysis covering required expenses and revenue. | Satisfactory analysis covering all aspects. | Basic level of analysis. | — |
| Quality of the report | Very good. | Decent report and presentation. | Basic report and presentation. | — |
| Unique aspects (patent / publishable / VC-presentable) | Significant. | Medium. | Basic solution. | — |
